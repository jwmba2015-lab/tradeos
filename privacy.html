Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

TradeOS is a personal trading dashboard built for individual use. This policy explains how the app handles your data.

Data storage

All data processed by TradeOS is stored locally on your device only — in your browser's localStorage. No data is transmitted to any external server, database, or third party. TradeOS has no backend server.

Whoop data

If you connect your Whoop account, TradeOS requests read-only access to the following data:

  • Recovery score
  • Sleep data
  • HRV (heart rate variability)
  • Physiological cycles

This data is fetched directly from the Whoop API to your device and stored only in your browser's localStorage. It is never sent anywhere else. You can disconnect Whoop at any time by clearing your browser data or using the disconnect option in the app.

Trading data

Trade files you upload (Webull exports) are processed entirely in your browser. No file contents are uploaded or transmitted anywhere.

Third-party services

TradeOS uses the following third-party services for display purposes only:

  • Google Fonts — for typography
  • Cloudflare CDN — for Chart.js library
  • Whoop API — for biometric data (only when you choose to connect)

No tracking

TradeOS contains no analytics, no tracking pixels, no advertising, and no cookies beyond what is required for the Whoop OAuth authentication flow.

Data deletion

To delete all stored data, clear your browser's localStorage for this site, or use the Reset button within the app.

Contact

This is a personal application. For questions, manage your Whoop API access at developer.whoop.com.
