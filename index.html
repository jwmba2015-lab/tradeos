Connect your Whoop
Connect once and your recovery, HRV, and sleep data syncs automatically every time you open TradeOS. Takes about 60 seconds.
1
Tap the button below
You'll be taken to Whoop's secure login page. Sign in with your regular Whoop email and password.
2
Tap "Allow" on the Whoop permissions screen
TradeOS requests read-only access to your recovery, sleep, HRV, and cycles. It can never modify your Whoop data.
3
You'll be brought back here automatically
TradeOS pulls your last 90 days of data and syncs it with your trades. Your token is saved — you won't need to log in again.
4
Upload your Webull trades if you haven't already
Go to the Home tab and upload your Webull CSV. TradeOS will then match your trading days with your Whoop data automatically.
